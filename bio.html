Early life and education

I grew up in Redmond, Washington and earned an undergraduate degree from the University of Washington. I majored in Communication, Sociology and Swedish, with a minor in Diversity. In the summer of 2015, I studied Swedish abroad with the Uppsala International Summer Session (UISS).

What is the UISS?

The Uppsala International Summer Session is a program for students and professionals who are interested in taking intensive Swedish courses during the summer. For more information, you can check out their website here.

Professional life

I currently work in Seattle at Brooks Running in the eCommerce department.

Connection to Whidbey

My family has had a house on Whidbey Island for 15 years. I've experienced the island as a kid, teenager and adult and still find new things to explore each time I visit.
